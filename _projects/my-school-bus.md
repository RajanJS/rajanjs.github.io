## Context

My School Bus is a school transport tracking platform designed to improve child safety and daily transport coordination for parents, schools, and transport staff. The system combines GPS location, attendance-style tracking, and alerts so families and school operations teams can make better real-time decisions.

![Project Image](/assets/img/project/myschoolbus/my-school-bus.jpeg)

## My Role

I contributed to the mobile and web tracking workflows used by parents, school staff, transport managers, and bus attendants.

My contributions included:

- implementing real-time GPS and attendance-linked visibility flows
- building notification workflows for pickup, drop-off, delay, and route events
- supporting route planning and re-routing behavior for school operations
- contributing to role-specific experiences for parent, transport manager, and attendant use cases
- helping productize hardware-assisted tracking with operational web tooling

## Technical Challenges

- ensuring live location and event updates remained useful in day-to-day transport operations
- designing alerts that were timely enough to improve safety without causing notification fatigue
- supporting multiple user roles with different priorities and workflows
- balancing map-heavy interfaces with performance and usability on mobile devices
- integrating hardware-backed tracking with application-level reliability

## Outcomes

- Improved transport visibility for parents and schools through real-time location and event updates
- Reduced manual coordination by digitizing attendance and trip-status communication
- Enabled more proactive transport planning through route, speed, and schedule insights
- Supported safer and more predictable school commute experiences for students

## Extra Feature Details

- Parent, transport manager, and bus attendant app workflows supported
- alerting coverage includes pickup/drop events, route deviation, and speed monitoring
- timing-based alerts included an advance reminder window before pickup/drop schedules
- RFID + GPS integrations supported student boarding/alighting visibility
- hardware workflow was designed around a compact single-unit tracking/reader setup
- hardware manufacturing and reliability support included local collaboration with Spark Innovation
